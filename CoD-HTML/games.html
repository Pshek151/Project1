Call of Duty Main article: Call of Duty (video game) Call of Duty is a video game based on the Quake III Arena engine (id Tech 3), and was released on October 29, 2003. Black Ops story

Call of Duty: Black Ops Main article: Call of Duty: Black Ops Call of Duty: Black Ops is the seventh installment in the series,[21][22][23] the third developed by Treyarch, and was published by Activision for release on November 9, 2010. It is the first game in the series to take place during the Cold War and also takes place partially in the Vietnam War. It was initially available for Microsoft Windows, Xbox 360, and PlayStation 3 and was later released for the Wii as well as the Nintendo DS.
